About Me

Hi, I'm Bagus, an IoT Developer passionate about bridging the gap between the physical and digital worlds. My journey in technology, with a concentration in the Internet of Things, has equipped me with the skills to turn everyday objects into smart, connected systems that solve real-world problems.

As an Embedded Systems Engineer, I enjoy working at the hardware level to build robust and efficient solutions. However, I believe that even the most advanced technology is only as good as its user interface. That's why I am also a UI/UX Enthusiast, dedicated to designing intuitive dashboards and applications that make complex IoT systems accessible and easy to use for everyone.

When I'm not developing code or soldering components, you'll find me exploring the latest microcontrollers, sketching out new system architectures, or simply imagining how connected technology can create a more efficient and sustainable future.
